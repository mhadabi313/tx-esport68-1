Road To Pro

Structured Training

Weekly practice blocks combine aim routines, tactical VOD review, and role-focused reps so each player improves one concrete skill every session.

Tournament Readiness

Before official matches, the roster runs pressure simulations and internal scrims to sharpen communication, confidence, and decision speed.

Long-Term Growth

TX is built around consistency across seasons, with steady improvement plans for mechanics, teamwork, and adaptability in changing metas.
